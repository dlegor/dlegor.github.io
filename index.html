Hello, I'm Daniel Legorreta. With over a decade of experience spanning Data Engineering, Data Science, and Machine Learning Engineering, I focus on building complete, end-to-end AI systems. From developing national policy-changing NLP models to architecting highly scalable vector search engines and document extraction pipelines, I am passionate about leveraging data to solve complex real-world problems.

My core expertise includes:

  • Advanced NLP and LLMs for large-scale information retrieval and text comparison.
  • Scalable Forecasting models in distributed environments (Spark, Dask).
  • High-impact Anomaly Detection and Risk Analysis systems.
  • Computer Vision systems for Document Digitization and Information Extraction.

I constantly explore new techniques—from cutting-edge Deep Learning architectures to robust cloud deployments (AWS, GCP). I also enjoy sharing my knowledge through teaching and giving mentoring talks.
