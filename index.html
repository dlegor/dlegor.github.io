This is my personal page, my name is Daniel Legorreta. He often works on end-to-end machine learning projects (lifecycle in ML), sometimes as a data engineer, sometimes as a data scientist, and more recently as a ML engineer. Because I have worked on many projects, I have been fortunate to use many technologies,methodologies and work in different roles on ML projects. I like data projects, but I love building systems with ML, not just models, dashboards, or pipelines. In general, I work on three topics, implementation of NLP for different business cases, implementation of forecasting models in distributed or non-distributed systems, and implementation of models for anomaly detection.

I have always liked learning and researching new techniques or algorithms and trying them out on a personal project. I also enjoy sharing what I learn, giving talks, courses in schools or work teams.
