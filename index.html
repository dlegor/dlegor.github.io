Hello, I'm Daniel Legorreta, and this is my personal page. My work centers on end-to-end machine learning projects (ML lifecycle), where I contribute my skills as a data engineer, data scientist, and machine learning engineer. This diverse experience has allowed me to gain proficiency in a wide array of technologies and methodologies and to operate effectively in different roles within ML projects. I am passionate about constructing robust machine learning systems that encompass more than just models, dashboards, or pipelines. My key areas of focus include the implementation of:

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) for diverse business applications(LLMs aplications).
  • Forecasting models in distributed and non-distributed computing environments.
  • Anomaly detection models.
  • Segmentation and Object Detection models

    I have always liked learning and researching new techniques or algorithms and trying them out on a personal project. I also enjoy sharing what I learn, giving talks, courses in schools or work teams.
